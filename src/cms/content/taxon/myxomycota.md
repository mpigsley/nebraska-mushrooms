*Myxomycota* is a group of slime molds that live in damp forest litter, logs, and soil, especially after rain. Though not fungi, they form similar spore-producing structures. They begin as microscopic amoeba-like cells that merge into a visible, often brightly colored slime stage called a plasmodium.
