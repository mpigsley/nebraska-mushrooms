_Lanmaoa_ is a genus of boletes characterized by a shallow hymenophore in comparison to other bolete genera. A quick field test for this genus is to determine the ratio of the hymenophore height (`H.Y`) to the pileus context height (`PctY`).

If `H.Y` is 1/5 to 1/3 (one-fifth to one-third) the size of `PctY`, there's a chance your bolete is _Lanmaoa_ or a _Baorangia_. This test must be measured from the halfway point from the cap center to cap margin (Wu et al., 2016).
