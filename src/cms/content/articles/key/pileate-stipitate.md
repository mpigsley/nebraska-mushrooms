P = Pileus (cap, hat, head, sombrero)<br>
H = Hymenophore (gills, pores, folds, teeth)<br>
S = Stipe (stem, stalk, foot, toe)<br>
M = Basal Mycelium 
</div>

<hr>

Fungi that fit into this group have a cap (pileus) and stem (stipe). For all of these fungi, the hymenophore, is synonymous to "what is under the cap".
