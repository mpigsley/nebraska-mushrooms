- Growing gregariously from soil in mixed oak/hickory woodland.
- Nearby trees: Black Oak, Bur Oak, Elm, Northern Red Oak and distant Shagbark Hickory.

Smell: not distinctive.
Taste: quickly acrid (spicy).
