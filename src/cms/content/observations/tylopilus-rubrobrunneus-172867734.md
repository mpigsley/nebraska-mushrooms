#192

Growing in open mixed oak/hickory woodland edge.

- Nearby trees: Black Oak, Bur Oak, Chinkapin Oak and distant Northern Red Oak and Ash.
- Pileipellis finely velvety with enrolled margin when young.
- Stipe with reticulation at apex.
- Hymenium white, turning similar color to cap when bruised with knife.
- Flesh unchanging when sliced.
- Smell: Faintly of burnt rubber.
- Taste: Very Bitter.
