Collected With No Number.

**Richardson Co. Nebraska**

Hypogeous, roughly 4-5 cm down in rodent dog site, in upland mixed oak/hickory woodland.

Microscopy: spores mounted in Melzer's reagent. Outer peridium rehydrated in ammonia and mounted in Congo Red.
