#377

Growing gregariously among open moss bed on mixed oak woodland edge.

- Stroma black, club shaped, covered in perithecia at the top with a tapered non fertile portion extending into the ground.
- Rhizomorphs underground goldish in color, connecting to an ornamented species of Elaphomyces.
