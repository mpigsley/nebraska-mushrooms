#355

- Hypogeous in open mixed oak/hickory woodland ridge.
- Nearby Trees: Shagbark Hickory, Chinkapin Oak, Ash, American Hophornbeam, Black Oak, and Black Walnut.
- Exoperidium thin and tan.
- Interior powdery and black with whitish inner fibrous material.
- Smell: foul
- Microscopy: mounted in Melzer's. Spores ornamented in dense layer of curled spines. (Reminiscent of Scleroderma)
