AA21

- Growing clustered from soil bank near creek in Bur Oak dominated woodland draw.
- Nearby Trees: Bur Oak, Eastern Red Cedar, Elm, and White Mulberry.
- Latex cream/buff colored, similarly to lamellae.

- Smell: not distinctive
- Taste: pleasant and mild
- Spore Print: light buff
- Microscopy: Mounted in Melzer's Reagent. Spores amyloid, highly ornamented with tall ridge-like spines that create a reticulate pattern.
