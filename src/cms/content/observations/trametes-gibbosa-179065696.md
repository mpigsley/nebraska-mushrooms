#327

- Growing gregariously on fallen American Sycamore in low, open riparian woodland area.
- Cap lumpy, concentrically zoned, alternating between white, cream, and gray violet. Some specimens with green algal component.
- Hymenium poroid, elongated, irregular and mazelike.

Additional Info
- White spore deposit on log.
- KOH darkening pileipellis and hymenium.
- Ammonia darkening pileipellis and hymenium.
