#354

- Hypogeous in mixed oak/hickory woodland ridge.
- Nearby Trees: Shagbark Hickory, Chinkapin Oak, Ash, American Hophornbeam, Black Oak, and Black Walnut.
- Peridium gray and fairly thick.
- Interior laden with powdery light colored spores.
- Smell: slightly foul.
- Taste: not distinctive
-  Microscopy: mounted in Melzer's.
