#126

Emerging from large decaying hardwood log in the bottom of low moist woodland draw dominated by paw paw trees. Substrate appears to be insect larvae. Diptera fly larvae?

Abundant on other hardwood logs of similar degradation nearby. 
Many specimens on the log with secondary fungal infection (hyperparasitism?) 

Taste: Stroma was not distinctive while the host was increasingly bitter.
