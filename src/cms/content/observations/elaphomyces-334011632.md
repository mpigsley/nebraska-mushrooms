Collected With No Number.

**Nemaha Co. Nebraska**

Hypogeous in upland mixed oak/hickory woodland slope.
Nearby Trees: Black Oak, Shagbark Hickory, Black Walnut, Bitternut Hickory, Pawpaw, American Hop Hornbeam, Northern Red Oak, and Elm.

UV: endoperidium light green under 365nm light.

Microscopy: first series of shots mounted in Melzer's reagent and last 4 shots mounted in Congo Red.
