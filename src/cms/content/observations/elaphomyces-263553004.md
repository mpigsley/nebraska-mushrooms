AA89

Hypogeous, roughly 3-4 cm deep in the side wall of a rodent dig site, in upland mixed oak/hickory woodland.

- Nearby Trees: Black Oak, Chinkapin Oak, Basswood, Shagbark Hickory, and Black Cherry.
- Exoperidium thin, brown and hard with a finely wrinkled surface.
- Endoperidium thick and gray.
- Gleba whitish and powdery.
- Smell: not distinctive from the dirt it was buried in.

- Microscopy: mounted in Melzer's Reagent.
