#378

- Hypogeous, roughly 3-4 cm below moss patches in open mixed oak woodland edge.
- Parasitized by Tolypocladium ophioglossoides.
- Nearby Trees: Black Oak, American Hophornbeam, Ash, Northern Red Oak, and Shagbark Hickory.
- Outer surfaces highly ornamented with tiny polyhedral spines.
