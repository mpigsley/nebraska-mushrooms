The American Blusher (_Amanita amerirubescens_) is a mycorrhizal mushroom that can be found in Oak and Pine-dominated woods mostly during the summer but also into early fall. It is a gilled mushroom that can be found in the soil, has large membranous skirt, and has a bulbous base without a volva. It stains red and has a white spore print.

This species hasn't been described formally in North America yet, and is under the provisional name _Amanita "rubescens-02"_.
