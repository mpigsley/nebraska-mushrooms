The Snow Fungus (_Tremella fuciformis_) is a jelly fungus that grows on hardwood logs after heavy rains. It fruits from the summer through fall. It can sometimes become parasitized by [_Sporothrix epigloea_](/species/sporothrix-epigloea/).
