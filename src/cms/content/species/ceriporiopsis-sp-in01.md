_Ceriporiopsis sp-IN01_ is a polypore originally found on decayed hardwood in southern Indiana. Species of _Ceriporiopsis_ are characterized by resupinate, soft fruiting bodies with pale, round to angular pores and are known for decomposing dead wood and causing white rot.
