The Lynx Paw Oyster (_Pleurotus levis_) is a decomposer of wood that can be found in the spring through fall. It can become quite large compared to other [_Pleurotus sp._](/taxa/pleurotus). It is colored white to cream to light yellow. The flesh bruises yellow when damaged or cut. The gills are cream to yellow colored and decurrent. The spore print is white.
