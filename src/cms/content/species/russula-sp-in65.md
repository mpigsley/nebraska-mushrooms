_Russula sp-IN65_ is a mycorrhizal mushroom that can be found in the summer in woodland settings. This one was found in an Oak/Hickory woodland growing from a moss patch near American Hophornbeam, Black Oak, Northern Red Oak, Chinkapin Oak and Black Cherry trees.
