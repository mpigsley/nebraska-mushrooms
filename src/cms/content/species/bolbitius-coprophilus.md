The Dung-Loving Bolbitius (_Bolbitius coprophilus_) is a widespread gilled mushroom that grows on... (you guessed it) dung! It can also be found on compost, and other nitrogen-rich organic substrates. It grows alone or in groups (singular or gregarious). Its gills are slightly attached to the stem (acutely adnexed). _B. coprophilus_ has a brownish spore print (dull cinnamon brown).
