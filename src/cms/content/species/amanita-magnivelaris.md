This is a deadly poisonous mushroom. Please see [Deadly Mushrooms of Nebraska](https://www.nebmyco.com/blog/2021-02-15-deadly-mushrooms-of-nebraska/) by C. Brueggemann where it is treated as _Amanita bisporigera_.

The Great Felt Skirt Destroying Angel is a mycorrhizal mushroom that grows in soil in wooded habitats in the summer. It is white, has a membranous skirt on the stem, and has a bulbous base with a volva.
