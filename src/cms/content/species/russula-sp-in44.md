_Russula sp-IN44_ is a mycorrhizal mushroom that can be found in the summer in woodland settings. This one was found in an Oak/Hickory woodland near Red Mulberry, Northern Red Oak, Eastern Red Bud, Chinkapin Oak, and Elm trees at Indian Cave State Park. It has cross-veins in the gills, smells mealy, and tastes peppery/spicy (acrid).
