The Pink Fringed Faery Cup (_Microstoma floccosum_) is a cup fungus that can be found in the summer growing from partially submerged fallen wood. It has a pink cup or goblet shape on the end of a long white stem. It is covered in long hairs that join into pointed bundles. The hairs are prominent towards the margin of the cup.
