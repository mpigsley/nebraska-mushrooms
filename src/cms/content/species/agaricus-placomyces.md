The Flat-Top Champion (_Agaricus placomyces_) is a decomposer that can be found in soil from summer through fall. It can be found in mixed woods. This species of _Agaricus_ grow quite large and mature caps can be flat (plane) with a darker-colored center. The gills start pinkish colored and turn to dark brown with age. It has a brown spore print.
